PIXEL TO REM CONVERSION

1 REM equals 16 pixels. For example, if a paragraph in your HTML document has a size that's 1.5 pixels, it'll be 24 pixels, which is 1.5 times your root font size.

PERCENTAGE TO EM CONVERSION

1em = 100%, 2em = 200%, 1,4 em = 140% and so on. However, it's actually contex dependent. 1em means "equeal to the actual font-size", 2 - "2 times the font-size". EMs adapt to users settings.
